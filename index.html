15TH ANNUAL CHINATOWN COMMUNITY FOUNDATION GOLF TOURNAMENT

Wednesday, October 11, 2023

Hoakalei Country Club

9:30 AM Check-in

11:00 AM Shotgun Start

Please join us for a day of golf, fun and networking at the beautiful and exclusive Hoakalei Country Club. Proceeds will help to fund several scholarships and grants for students and schools in our community as well as help the foundation to carry out its mission: improving downtown and Chinatown for our community and its merchants.

Gather your friends, make a team of 4, and enjoy a relaxing and fun time with the chance to win fabulous prizes! If you're not a golfer, we could use your support through a donation or as a volunteer during the tournament.

*Includes 1 lucky number ticket per player, pre-tournament putting contest, longest drive contest and closest to the pin contests

DONATIONS

Happy Year of the Rabbit! The Chinatown Community Foundation, supported by the Chinese Chamber of Commerce of Hawaii is holding our Annual Golf Tournament on October 11, 2023, at the beautiful Hoakalei Country Club. We are expecting over 144 golfers!

We humbly ask for your support to provide donations which will be used in the lucky number giveaway or our Golfers Welcome Bag. This can include gift certificates, discount cards, golf balls, pens, samples, etc. Anything that can assist in promoting your company.

Joining us this year will be many community business leaders, so it is truly an excellent opportunity to meet and network to both help grow your business and support the local community in Hawaii.

The proceeds from our Annual Golf Tournament continue to help fund student scholarships, business educational programs and services, and efforts to clean up and revitalize Downtown and Chinatown, while promoting the goals of Chinatown Community Foundation.

On behalf of the Chinese Chamber of Commerce of Hawaii and Chinatown Community Foundation, Mahalo Nui Loa for your support!

All gifts are tax deductible under 501(c)(3) tax identification number: 99-0315441

EDUCATIONAL SCHOLARSHIP AND TEACHER GRANT

This year, theChinatown Community Foundation (CCF) established an Educational Scholarship & Teacher Grant Program to support furthering education of our young adults and also support educators in our local community. The awards are as follows:

Two (2) Educational Scholarship Awards* - $1,000/award

Two (2) Teacher/School Grant Awards - $500/award

The application for the Educational Scholarship Award will be open from August 01, 2022, to September 30, 2022. The completed application must be submitted in its entirety by September 30, 2022. All documents should be submitted in PDF format via email totge@chinesechamber.comno later than the end of the business day (4:00 pm).

*Only high school, post-secondary, and college students may apply for the educational scholarship.
